This editor allows only Clifford gates.
  • Drag & drop single-qubit gates onto the circuit (they will be placed in the nearest free column).
  • For 2Q gates: click the 2Q gates button, pick the gate, then click two qubits. For directed gates (CX/SWAP) you click control then target.
  • You can drag gates already placed to move them to a free slot.
  • Click a placed gate to remove it.
